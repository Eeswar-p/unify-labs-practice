About DevConf 2026

The premier technology conference bringing together innovators and leaders

Our Mission

DevConf 2026 is dedicated to advancing the technology industry through knowledge sharing, professional development, and community building. We bring together the brightest minds in technology to explore innovative solutions and discuss the future of software development.

Our conference provides a platform for developers, architects, DevOps engineers, and tech leaders to learn from industry experts, network with peers, and discover the latest technologies shaping our digital world.

With a focus on practical knowledge and hands-on experience, DevConf 2026 offers a diverse range of sessions, workshops, and networking events designed to accelerate your career and expand your professional network.

Code of Conduct

We're committed to creating a respectful and inclusive environment for all attendees

Our Commitment

DevConf 2026 is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices.

Expected Behavior

  • Treat all attendees with respect and kindness
  • Be professional and inclusive in all interactions
  • Respect the intellectual property and privacy of others
  • Participate respectfully in discussions and debates
  • Help create a welcoming environment for everyone

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, bullying, or discriminatory behavior
  • Offensive language or jokes targeting protected characteristics
  • Unwanted advances or attention of a sexual nature
  • Recording or sharing content without consent
  • Disruption of sessions or events

For the complete Code of Conduct and reporting procedures, please visit our detailed Code of Conduct page.
