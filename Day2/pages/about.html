About DevConf 2026

DevConf 2026 is a premier professional development conference bringing together the brightest minds in technology and business innovation. Our mission is to foster knowledge sharing, collaboration, and growth within the global professional community.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To create an inclusive, inspiring environment where professionals can learn from industry leaders, connect with peers, and discover the latest innovations that are shaping the future of technology and business.

Vision

To become the premier destination for professional development and networking, recognized globally for bringing together diverse perspectives and fostering meaningful connections that drive innovation and career growth.

What We Offer

Expert Keynotes

Learn from thought leaders and industry pioneers who share their insights on the latest trends and innovations in technology, business strategy, and professional development.

Hands-On Workshops

Participate in interactive workshops led by experts, covering topics from cloud architecture and AI implementation to cybersecurity and DevOps practices. These sessions provide practical skills you can apply immediately in your work.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with professionals from around the world. Our dedicated networking events, social activities, and online community provide multiple ways to build meaningful professional relationships.

Professional Development

Earn recognized certifications and professional development credits. Our conference is approved for continuing education credits in many professional organizations.

Diverse Formats

Choose how you want to participate: in-person, virtual, or hybrid. We provide flexibility so you can attend in the way that works best for your schedule and needs.

Our History

DevConf was founded in 2018 with a simple goal: to bring together professionals who are passionate about learning, innovation, and growth. What started as a regional conference with 500 attendees has grown into a premier international event.

Growth Timeline

  • 2018: First DevConf with 500 attendees
  • 2019: Expansion to 1,500 attendees with 30+ workshops
  • 2020: Virtual conference during global pandemic - 3,000 participants
  • 2021: Hybrid format introduced - 3,500 attendees
  • 2022: Return to in-person with expanded venue - 4,000 attendees
  • 2023: International expansion - 4,500 attendees from 40 countries
  • 2024: Record attendance - 5,000+ attendees and 200+ speakers
  • 2026: Continued growth and innovation with 5,000+ expected attendees
