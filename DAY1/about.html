About DevConf 2026

Our Mission

DevConf is committed to fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the global technology community. Our mission is to provide a platform where developers, engineers, and technology leaders can come together to explore emerging trends, share best practices, and shape the future of software development.

Since our inception in 2010, DevConf has grown from a small gathering of 200 local developers to an international conference attracting thousands of participants from over 50 countries. We pride ourselves on creating an inclusive environment that welcomes professionals at all career stages, from students and junior developers to CTOs and tech entrepreneurs.

Each year, we carefully curate our program to reflect the most relevant and impactful topics in technology. Our speaker selection process is rigorous, ensuring that attendees receive insights from true experts who are actively shaping their respective fields. From hands-on technical workshops to strategic keynotes, DevConf offers something valuable for everyone.

Beyond the sessions and workshops, DevConf is renowned for its networking opportunities. We facilitate connections that lead to collaborations, job opportunities, and lasting professional relationships. Many attendees return year after year, making DevConf not just a conference, but a community.

Why Attend DevConf 2026?

  • Learn from industry-leading experts and innovators
  • Gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies
  • Network with peers and potential employers from top tech companies
  • Discover career advancement opportunities and mentorship
  • Stay ahead of industry trends and emerging technologies
  • Participate in interactive workshops and live coding sessions
  • Access exclusive sponsor exhibitions and product demos

Who Should Attend?

DevConf 2026 is designed for professionals across the technology spectrum:

  1. Software Developers and Engineers
  2. DevOps and Site Reliability Engineers
  3. Data Scientists and Machine Learning Engineers
  4. Technical Architects and Team Leads
  5. CTOs, VPs of Engineering, and Technology Directors
  6. Product Managers working with technical teams
  7. Computer Science Students and Recent Graduates
  8. Technology Entrepreneurs and Startup Founders
